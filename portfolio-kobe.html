Kobe Store

Project Description

Kobe is an ecommerce specialized in anime and fiction merchandising. This store was created as a final project for Mindhub's FullStack development course.
This store has email verification.
You must be logged in to make a purchase. The store has an administration section with stock management, prices and massive price updates.
To buy you can create a new account or use our verified administrator account.
